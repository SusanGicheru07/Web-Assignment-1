About IEEE WIE

The IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) affinity group at Multimedia University of Kenya is dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests.

Our Mission

To empower women in engineering through mentorship, collaboration, leadership, and community engagement.

Our Vision

A world where diversity and inclusion drive innovation in engineering and technology.
